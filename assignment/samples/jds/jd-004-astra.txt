Astra - Software Engineer

The Opportunity
As a Software Engineer at Astra, you'll build systems that support rocket testing, manufacturing, and launch operations.

How You'll Fulfill Your Mission:
- Develop and maintain Python-based systems for automating rocket testing, manufacturing, and launch operations.
- Build tools for real-time telemetry data collection and analysis to support rapid decision-making.
- Contribute to internal tools, APIs (REST/gRPC), and cloud-native infrastructure that enable fast iteration and high reliability.

Why We Value You:
- 3+ years of experience in software engineering, including production-level Python development
- Solid understanding of system design, data pipelines, and APIs
- Experience with cloud-native services (e.g., AWS, Kubernetes, Docker) and telemetry or time-series systems
- Familiarity with REST/gRPC APIs and common data serialization formats (e.g., JSON, YAML, Protobuf)
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field

Desired Multipliers:
- Experience in aerospace, robotics, or hardware-software systems
- Exposure to TypeScript, Go, or C/C++
- Open-source contributions or relevant side projects

The pay range for this role is: $130,000 - $160,000 per year
